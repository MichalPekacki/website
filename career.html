After I leave university I am looking to become a computer games developer. My aspiration is to become an independent, also known as indie, games developer, this means I will work by myself or in a small team to create a game. This is because I will be able to have big impact on the game and have the freedom to be creative. In bigger companies that work on AAA games you have to work under the demand of the customer. This doesn’t mean I wouldn’t work for a big company such as Blizzard, this would give me experience and I would be able to learn of more people who have much more experience than me. With that experience I would then like to make my own game which hopefully will be as successful as other indie games such as Minecraft or World of Goo.
