Modern Warfare 2

Modern Warfare 2 is another game in the so popular Call of Duty series. In my opinion it is the best game I have ever played due to the friendly experience you get from the gameplay. Although with the technology we have right now the graphic of the game is not impressive I still love coming back to it and playing it. The campaign is good and the story has a good depth to it, however it is the multiplayer which makes this game so enjoyable.

Skyrim

Skyrim is another outstanding game with much depth and hours of gameplay. The combat system is not as good as, for example, Dragon's Dogma however it is still enjoyable. The game has a lot of content and the graphics are stunning. The game gives you choices and you are responsible for how you want to play the game. Endless hours of exploration, combat and quests await you at every moment, you simply cannot get bored.

Heroes of Might and Magic V

Heroes of Might and Magic is a strategic game where you choose a faction and explore the land to find resource essential for your development. The end goal is defeat other factions who oppose you so in order to do that you need to expand your faction. It is a very good turn base strategic game with beautiful environment and in depth gameplay.

Dark Souls

Dark Souls is one of the best games I have ever played, the combat in the game is very good and the game mechanics are very challenging. What I like about the game is the fact it is so challenging. Most games guide you through their world where as this game simply gives you a quick tutorial of controls, the rest is for you to figure out. With no maps or quick saving system it is very hard to progress. The story is mysterious and hard to understand but the game is very atmospheric.

Fifa 16

Fifa 16 is a football game. You control you team and try to score against the opposition. What is so good about the game is its challenging mechanics and the different types of play. You can choose to play a simply quick game, make a player or manager career or play ultimate team where you build your team in a card trading manner. The career mode is very in depth and endless which gives you hours of gameplay.
